About Us

Santa Clara Glass Inc. specializes in the design, fabrication, procurement, and installation of custom glass for residential, commercial, metal storefront facades for luxury retail, high-end commercial construction projects and consumer designer glass works in heart of Silicon Valley.

We differentiate ourselves from our competition by offering a full range of in-house services including design, consultation, budget development, detail development, and custom fabricating.

SCG continually researches new products and suppliers, and is committed to providing our clients with the most cutting-edge glazing products sourced globally. We value our strong client relationships and we nurture those relationships through open communication and outstanding service.
